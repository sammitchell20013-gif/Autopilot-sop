## üöÄ Next Steps to Get on Google:

### **Step 1: Submit Your Site to Google Search Console**

1. Go to: https://search.google.com/search-console
2. Click **"Add Property"**
3. Enter your domain: `autopilotsop.com`
4. Choose verification method:
   - **Recommended**: HTML tag (I'll show you where to add it)
   - Or use domain registrar DNS verification

#### HTML Tag Verification:
Google will give you a tag like:
```html
<meta name="google-site-verification" content="YOUR_CODE_HERE" />
```

Add this to your site (I can help you add it to the layout).

5. Click **"Verify"**

### **Step 2: Submit Your Sitemap**

Once verified:
1. In Google Search Console, go to **"Sitemaps"** (left sidebar)
2. Enter: `sitemap.xml`
3. Click **"Submit"**

Google will now crawl your site regularly!

---

### **Step 3: Index Your Homepage Manually (Faster)**

1. In Google Search Console, go to **"URL Inspection"** (top)
2. Enter: `https://autopilotsop.com`
3. Click **"Request Indexing"**

This tells Google to crawl your homepage immediately instead of waiting.
